## üåå Motivation

Efforts such as SETI/METI typically transmit simple mathematical sequences (e.g., primes) to demonstrate intelligence.  
However, these signals do **not** communicate:

- whether the sender is a *living organism*,  
- what their biological structure is,  
- or the *nature of their technological sophistication*.

This project proposes that **a universal language must express both**:
1. **Technological constructs** (computation, machines, logic)  
2. **Biological constructs** (growth, recursion, behavior)

PFUL attempts to meet this need by unifying two fundamental number systems that exist independently of culture, biology, or evolution.

PTL is a formal language where:

- **Grammar = rewriting rules over prime compositions**

Using G√∂del-style encoding, PTL can represent:

- machine instructions

## üå± Fibonacci Biological Language (FBL)

FBL is a formal language for biological expression, where:

- **Organisms are represented as sequences of Fibonacci numbers**  
- **Behaviors are morphisms between Fibonacci sequences**  
- **Biological grammar consists of recursive growth rules**  

Fibonacci recursion appears widely in:

- morphogenesis  
- branching structures  
- scaling laws  
- resource-optimized biological growth  

FBL thus captures universal aspects of life **independent of biochemistry or planetary environment**.
